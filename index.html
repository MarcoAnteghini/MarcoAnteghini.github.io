Projects

Rare Disease Risk Assessment Platform

Development of an open-access computational platform for evaluating and predicting pathogenic genetic variants, with a focus on rare neurodevelopmental diseases. The project integrates genomic data analysis with machine learning to optimize variant pathogenicity prediction methods.

Human Gut Microbiota Analysis Pipeline

Design and implementation of reproducible pipelines for taxonomic profiling of gut bacteria in patient samples. The workflow enables detection of dysbiosis and supports personalized nutritional interventions for disease prevention and health optimization.

BIOS — Sustainable Bio-Manufacturing

Contribution to the BIOS Horizon Europe project, advancing sustainable bio-manufacturing through computational biology and bioinformatics approaches.

About Me

I am a passionate and experienced bioinformatics consultant with a robust background in both academic and corporate environments. I specialize in developing advanced bioinformatics pipelines for analyzing various kinds of biomedical data, including protein sequences, genomic and nutrigenomic data, microbial data, and clinical data. With a PhD in Bioinformatics from Wageningen University and a Master's degree from the University of Bologna, my expertise spans various research areas, including deep learning-based protein sequence analysis and AI-driven decision support systems. I thrive in team settings and enjoy tackling complex projects to deliver innovative solutions. Currently, I am driving bioinformatics research and operational management at LifeGlimmer GmbH while also contributing to personalized nutrition research at NGB Genetics. Additionally, I am working as a bioinformatics researcher for the BioFold group at the University of Bologna on a project aiming to unravel the genomic information of rare diseases.

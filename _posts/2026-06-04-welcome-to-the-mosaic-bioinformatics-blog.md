This is a space for practical writing on bioinformatics, machine learning, and reproducible data work.

<!--more-->

## What to expect

Short, useful pieces on the kind of problems I work on day to day:

- Building reproducible pipelines that survive past the engagement
- Machine learning on biological sequences, variants, and microbiome data
- Turning messy lab and administrative data into clear decisions
- Notes and ideas from the [BioIntelligent podcast](https://biointelligent.riverside.com/)

## Not only my writing

Alongside my own notes, this blog will also feature guest articles and contributions from collaborators and others working on related problems. If you'd like to contribute a piece, [get in touch](/#contact).

This is the new home for my writing on bioinformatics, machine learning, and reproducible data work. Until now I've shared most of these notes on LinkedIn. From here on, I'll publish them here first, then repost them there.

<!--more-->

## Why write here

LinkedIn is great for reach, but posts are hard to find again and the formatting is limiting. Owning the content means I can write longer, link properly, embed code, and keep an archive that's easy to browse.

## What to expect

Expect short, practical pieces on the kind of work I do day to day:

- Building reproducible pipelines that survive past the engagement
- Machine learning on biological sequences, variants, and microbiome data
- Turning messy administrative and lab data into decisions
- Notes from the [BioIntelligent podcast](https://biointelligent.riverside.com/)

If a topic here is useful to you or your team, [get in touch](/#contact). Thanks for reading.
